About Us

Defining railing industry standards for over three decades

MAB & Associates North is a second-generation, woman-led firm that specializes in furnishing and installing decorative railings, louvers, and other architectural metals. Since 1987, we have provided architectural solutions by delivering custom decorative steel, aluminum, and glazed glass products.

With an architect-led team and a commitment to proactive problem-solving, we collaborate with customers to deliver the results they need in the timeframe they demand. Our focus on identifying potential issues early prevents problems during the construction process.
